# Sumtotal

A small logic puzzle (Sumplete-style): delete numbers so each row and column adds up to the target shown at the right and bottom.

**Play:** https://dev.jdayers.com/sumtotal/

## How to play

- Click a number to mark it for deletion (✕); click again to circle it (keep); click once more to clear.
- A row/column target turns dark when those numbers add up correctly. Solve every row and column to win.
- **Errors** flags wrong marks · **Hint** reveals one deletion · **Reveal** shows the solution · **Restart** clears your marks · **New Puzzle** generates a fresh board (3×3 up to 9×9).

Progress saves in your browser. Vanilla HTML/CSS/JS — no build, no dependencies.
